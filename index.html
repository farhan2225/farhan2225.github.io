About Me

Engineer. Leader.

I am Mohamed Farhan Jafarullah, a DevOps Engineer at Bosch Global Software Technologies in Coimbatore. I live at the intersection of infrastructure and intelligence — deploying GenAI on Kubernetes, automating systems, and building things that just work.

I served as Group Rotaract Representative overseeing 20 clubs across RI District 3201, and as Chair for all District Events. Before that I led the Rotaract Club of Coimbatore CACIL as President, delivering 115+ projects under "Fuelled To Fascinate."

My family owns Hotel Chanma International, a 3-star property in Coimbatore housing Ganis Gym & CrossFit and a newly launched rooftop swimming pool. I manage marketing, brand strategy, and digital presence for all our business verticals.

I train as a Hybrid Athlete combining weightlifting, CrossFit, cycling, badminton, and road racing. Pursuing an MBA in Marketing from Manipal Academy while working full-time at Bosch.
